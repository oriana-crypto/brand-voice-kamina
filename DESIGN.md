## Overview

Tech-Forward Fintech meets Bold Editorial Display. The UI evokes a confident,
data-driven product — a dark, immersive canvas energized by a vibrant violet glow.

## Colors

The palette is rooted in a deep purple darkscape with a luminous violet accent.

- **Primary (#9B5BF6):** Vibrant violet — the core accent for buttons and CTAs.
- **Secondary (#B43FF8):** Electric purple — highlights, links, and emphasis.
- **Tertiary (#190030):** Deep aubergine — the immersive page foundation.
- **Neutral (#FFFFFF):** Pure white for headlines and primary text on dark surfaces.

## Typography

Display headlines use **Bebas Neue** — tall, condensed, and uppercase — for
journalistic impact (h1 up to 96px / 6rem with ~2.4px letter spacing). Body copy
and UI labels use **Montserrat**, with bold (700) weight reserved for accent
sub-headings and emphasis.
